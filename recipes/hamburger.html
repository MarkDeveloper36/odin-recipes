Hamburger

Description

Best Hamburger Patty Recipe, made on the grill or stovetop, this is the best and easiest all-purpose recipe for perfect hamburger patties every time! These juicy, delicious homemade hamburgers are ready in less than 30 minutes and are a must-make for your next cookout.

Close your eyes, and picture the perfect hamburger. A thick steakhouse-sized burger, seared to the peak of juiciness on a grill, with melted cheese! Make burgers and have them come out flawless every time with what I consider to be the Best Hamburger Patty Recipe. It's a classic all-American recipe for mouthwatering burgers that can be cooked on the grill, as thick 1/3 pound patties, or as ultra-thin griddle patties.

Ingredients

  • Ground chuck
  • Panko bread crumbs
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Salt, Garlic Powder, Onion powder, Pepper, or other spices of choice

Steps

It seriously only takes mere minutes to make restaurant-quality hamburgers from scratch. Prep your workspace and ingredients before getting started and you'll be whipping out hot plates from your home kitchen (or backyard) in no time!

  1. First, set out a large mixing bowl and add in the ground beef, crushed crackers, egg, Worcestershire sauce, milk, and spices.
  2. Next, press the meat down in the bowl, into an even disk. Use a knife to cut and divide the hamburger patty mixture into 6 - 1/3 pound grill or skillet patties, or 12 thin griddle patties.
  3. Then, preheat the grill or a skillet to medium heat, approximately 350-400 degrees F.
